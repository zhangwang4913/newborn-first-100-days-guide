Section 05

Newborn Hospital Plan Guide

Securing your newborn's coverage early is one of the most important financial steps a new parent can take. Apply from 15 days old — and if you hold an eligible maternity plan, the 100-day Guaranteed Insurability Benefit (GIB) lets you transfer coverage to your child without any medical underwriting.

CPF — MediSave Grant for Newborns
Locking in Insurability — The "Clean Slate"
Applying at 15 days old is the gold standard because at that age, most healthy babies have a completely clean medical record.
The Danger of Delay
Waiting until the 1- or 3-month checkup risks a paediatrician noting minor issues — jaundice, mild murmur, slow weight gain, or infant eczema.
The Impact
Even common, minor findings can cause insurers to defer the application or approve it with permanent exclusions.
Apply After 15 Days Old
Parents cannot enrol a newborn in an Integrated Shield Plan at birth. Once your child is past 15 days old, apply promptly to lock in their insurability — before any health conditions are documented in their medical records. Acting early gives your child the best chance of comprehensive, lifelong coverage.
Use This to Pay Premiums
$5,000
MediSave Grant for Newborns
Auto-credited to your newborn's CPF MediSave upon birth registration. Can be used directly to pay the annual IP main plan premium — no cash outlay needed.
Guaranteed Insurability Benefit (GIB) — via a Bundled Maternity Plan. If you hold an eligible maternity plan, coverage can be transferred seamlessly to your newborn — or you can select an eligible plan under the GIB — both without medical underwriting, if exercised within 100 days of birth. This means your child enjoys full coverage even if any condition or procedure is linked to a pre-existing condition. This is why maternity planning and newborn coverage go hand in hand.

Don't forget the Rider. An IP without a co-payment Rider still leaves you exposed to large deductibles and co-insurance. Note: Rider premiums must be paid in cash and cannot be drawn from MediSave.

Preschool Registration Success Guide
Planning Ahead — Before Your Baby Arrives

Singapore's most sought-after infant care and childcare centres have waitlists of 6 to 18 months. Many parents miss their preferred centre simply because they registered too late. The playbook: research before birth, register at multiple centres early, and have a back-up.

Anchor Operators
PCF Sparkletots and My First Skool have the most locations island-wide and offer the most subsidised rates. Register via their websites directly — waitlists open anytime.
Private Centres
Popular private centres like Mulberry Learning, Little Skool-House, or MindChamps often have 12–18 month waitlists. Register as early as the 2nd trimester.
Pro Tip
Register at 3–4 centres simultaneously. You can always withdraw — you cannot go back in time to join a waitlist.
